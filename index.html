About me

My name is François. I am a PhD student in deep learning under the supervision of Prof. Gilles Louppe at the University of Liège in Belgium. My research mainly consists in developing and applying deep learning methods to Bayesian inference problems in large-scale dynamical systems (oceans, atmospheres, ...). My work lies at the intersection of many subjects, most notably density estimation, generative modeling and inverse problems.
